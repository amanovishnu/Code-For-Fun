Who are we?

Reliable Helping Hands (RHH) is a non-profit organization initiated by a group of school students back in 2011, Delhi. It strives to extend help to the underprivileged section of the society by equipping them with basic necessities and training them with different skills to enhance their livelihoods.

Get Started!

Our vision

To be the leading organization for supporting the backward section of the society in the Indian Society.

Our mission

To empower the kids and youth who belong to the underprivileged sections, for the purpose of their overall development and future enhancement. Further, the organization aims at helping the elderly segment of people from all sections, by providing them with basic essentials required for their healthy and peaceful living. In addition, we intent to target our efforts towards activities and campaigns that can help reduce environmental hazards and promote clean and healthy lifestyles
